ACCPL Traininig Division

ACCPL Training Division is a unit of Adityaa Call Center Pvt Ltd (ACCPL) , Bangalore ACCPL has vast experience in running international and domestic call center . ACCPL was launched in 2001 catering to USA and UK clients on voice based calling. Starting from 100 seats in 2001, the company expanded to 175 seats in 2002. The first batch of Adityaa (ACCPL training Division) was trained by faculties from internationally renowned trainers KathySisk enterprises, California, USA. The company diversified into training exclusively catering to Call center and BPO for private students from 2006. Further job oriented training subjects like Tally ERP, HW and NW, Animation etc were added to the portfolio. ACCPL has trained about 7550+ candidates sponsored by Karnataka Govt and placed about 1810 candidates in various verticals.

ACCPL Training Division has been awarded ISO 9001:2008 for process improvement in MASS SCALE TRAINING PROGRAMS . The certificate has been awarded after a strict auditing by Universal Registrars.
