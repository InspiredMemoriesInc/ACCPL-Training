about us

WELCOME TO ACCPL

ACCPL Training Division follows policy to donate and dedicate its resources a part of its profits towards the economically poor students from different walks of life.

Donation to schools which cater to students from economically weaker families.

Donation of computers and accessories to the schools and trusts run for the poor.

Free training to aspiring students who cannot qualify the Govt.’s norms for free training.

Helping the unemployed youths to acquire jobs through ACCPL’s placement referral system.
